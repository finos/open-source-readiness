## Summary

The conversation delves into the challenges and experiences of fundraising in the tech industry, particularly in AI and open source. It highlights the importance of understanding the value of open source contributions in financial services and the collaborative efforts needed to navigate this landscape. The discussion also touches on the future of open source collaboration and the necessity of quantifying its benefits to organizations.

## Takeaways

- Open source contributions are often undervalued in financial services.
- Understanding the ROI of open source is crucial for organizations.
- Collaboration in open source can lead to significant cost savings and efficiency gains.
- The value of open source extends beyond immediate financial metrics.
- Organizations need to be aware of their open source dependencies.
